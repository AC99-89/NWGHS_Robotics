Even More RC Testing

The girl drives the robot even more and is now inspired to become a robotics engineer.

We invited our local middle schoolers to learn about our club. To do this, we created two mini robots, a remote controlled mover and a drone launcher, and let the middle schoolers use it for fun. We were able to inspire the kids to pursue robotics and engineering and we also empowered a few women to follow their dreams of engineering as well. We also potentially got a few potential new members of the team thanks to this outreach.
