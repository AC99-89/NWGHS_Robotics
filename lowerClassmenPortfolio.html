Underclassmen Projects

Every year, the underclassmen of the Robo Vikings team work on one or more projects as a team to improve their skills and to prepare them for the rigors of building, documenting, and coding the main competition robot. Projects have ranged from videogames, mini robots, photography portfolios, 3D printing projects, and many more.
