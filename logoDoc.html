NWGHS Logo Documentation

Our logo was created using a photo editor as well as AI development technology. We communicated with the AI until it gave us a good image. We used our photo editor to edit the image and remove anything we do not want, and then added some text and our team number. We were so proud of this logo that we placed it on our team outfit.
The robot in the logo is a hybrid of an automaton and an animatronic.
